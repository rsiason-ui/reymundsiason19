Terms & Conditions

At the heart of every successful partnership is clarity and trust. You need to know exactly what to expect when we work together. That's why I've built my service on a foundation of absolute professionalism, transparency, and security, ensuring you can delegate with complete confidence.

1. The Client Services Agreement: Clarity from Day One

Before any work begins, we will co-create and sign a comprehensive Client Services Agreement (CSA). This document is not fine print; it is our shared blueprint for success, designed to eliminate any ambiguity. It clearly outlines:

  • Scope of Work: A detailed description of the specific tasks, projects, and responsibilities I will manage for you. This ensures we are perfectly aligned on expectations from the outset.
  • Performance Metrics: How we will measure success (e.e., inbox zero maintained by EOD each day, 24-hour response time on assigned queries).
  • Communication Protocols: Our preferred channels (e.e., Slack, email, Voxer), scheduled check-in times, and expected response windows for each of us.
  • Core Working Hours: My standard availability within Philippine Standard Time (PST), with clear guidelines for urgent requests outside these hours.
  • Revision Policy: The process for requesting adjustments or revisions to completed work.

This agreement is a living document. We will review it regularly to ensure it evolves with your business needs.

2. Financial Transparency: Simplified & Secure Billing

There will never be any surprises or informal payment requests. My financial system is designed for simplicity and professionalism.

  • Official Invoicing: You will receive a detailed, itemized digital invoice for each billing cycle (typically monthly or quarterly). Each invoice will clearly list the services rendered, the hours dedicated (if applicable), and the total amount due.
  • Secure Payment Methods: All payments are processed through secure, international platforms like Wise, PayPal, or Stripe. These channels offer buyer protection and secure transaction processing. I will never ask for a direct bank transfer or payment through an unsecured method.
  • Clear Payment Terms: Invoices will specify a clear due date (e.e., Net 7 or Net 15) and any late payment policies, ensuring smooth cash flow for both of us.
  • No Hidden Fees: The price we agree upon is the price you pay. Any potential additional costs for premium software subscriptions or other tools will be discussed and approved by you in advance.

3. Jurisdiction & Legal Framework: The Security of Philippine Law

Our formal working relationship is governed by the clear and robust legal framework of the Republic of the Philippines.

  • Professional Standards: The Philippines has a world-renowned and mature outsourcing industry. The legal system provides a strong, predictable framework for professional service contracts, protecting both the service provider and the client.
  • Data Privacy Compliance: I strictly adhere to the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173), which is based on stringent international standards (like GDPR). This means your personal and business data is handled with the highest level of confidentiality and security. My systems are designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.
  • Clear Dispute Resolution: The CSA will include a clause outlining the steps for resolving any potential disputes, typically beginning with meditation and arbitration under Philippine jurisdiction. This provides a clear, fair, and professional path forward should any issues ever arise, though my primary goal is to ensure they never do.

4. Confidentiality & Data Security: Your Information is Protected

Your business secrets are safe with me. From the moment we sign our agreement, you have my unwavering commitment to confidentiality.

  • Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA): A comprehensive mutual NDA is integrated into our Client Services Agreement. This legally binds me to protect all your proprietary information, business strategies, client lists, and any other sensitive data.
  • Ethical Standards: I operate with unwavering integrity. I will never use or share your information for any purpose other than fulfilling my duties to you.
  • Secure Tools: I use password-managed, encrypted communication and project management tools (like LastPass, Signal, or standard industry platforms) to ensure all client data remains secure.

Your Peace of Mind is My Priority

My goal is to remove your administrative stress, not add to it. This structured, transparent, and legally sound framework is the bedrock of my service. It allows us to build a productive, long-term partnership where you can focus on growth, knowing the operational details are handled with professionalism and integrity.

Privacy Notice

Your trust is the foundation of our working relationship. I am deeply committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with the highest standards of care and confidentiality. This policy outlines how I collect, use, and safeguard your data, ensuring you always remain in control.

1. The Information I Collect: Minimizing Data for Maximum Respect

I adhere to the principle of data minimization, meaning I only collect information that is absolutely essential for us to work together effectively and fulfill my contractual obligations to you.

This typically includes:

  • Identifiers: Your full name, email address, and business name.
  • Contact & Billing Details: Your business address and other necessary details for invoicing and communication.
  • Business Information: Details about your business operations, goals, and clientele that are required to provide tailored support services.
  • Credentials: Passwords or access details to tools required for task execution (e.g., social media logins, email access). These are handled with extreme caution and stored exclusively in a secure, encrypted password manager. I strongly recommend using a secure sharing method and enabling 2-factor authentication (2FA) on all accounts for our mutual security.

2. How I Use Your Information: Purpose-Limited and Transparent

Your data is used solely for legitimate business purposes to ensure I can deliver the high-quality service you expect. This includes:

  • Service Delivery: To manage your inbox, schedule, and other tasks as outlined in our agreement.
  • Communication: To send you updates, invoices, and important information regarding our work together.
  • Improvement: To analyze non-personal, aggregated data to improve the efficiency and quality of my services.
  • Security: To verify your identity and protect our accounts from unauthorized access or fraud.

3. Your Rights: You Are in Control

You have complete control over your personal information. At any time, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data I hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask me to update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request the deletion of your personal data, subject to certain legal or contractual obligations that may require me to retain specific records (e.e., for invoicing purposes under Philippine law).
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data, such as for direct marketing.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact me directly via this site, and I will respond promptly.

4. Data Storage and Security: Protecting What You Entrust to Me

I implement robust technical and organizational measures to keep your data secure:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard protocols.
  • Secure Tools: I only use reputable, secure software and cloud services (like Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) that comply with major data protection regulations.
  • Access Control: Your data is accessible only to me and is protected by strong, unique passwords and two-factor authentication wherever possible.

5. Use of Cookies and Site Analytics

This website may use "cookies" – small text files stored on your device – to enhance your experience. These are used for essential functions and to collect non-personal, anonymized data (e.e., browser type, pages visited, time spent on site). This analytics data helps me understand how the site is used and where I can improve its performance and content. You can control cookie settings through your own browser preferences.

6. Third-Party Sharing: Partners in Confidentiality

I only share your data with a select group of trusted third-party service providers when necessary to perform business functions (e.e., using Calendly for scheduling, QuickBooks for invoicing, or Google Workspace for file storage). Each provider is rigorously vetted and is bound by strict confidentiality and data processing agreements to ensure they protect your information to the same standard I do. I never sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third parties for marketing or any other purposes.

7. Contact Me

Your privacy is of utmost importance. If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please do not hesitate to reach out to me directly via the contact form on this site.

Intellectual Property

A successful partnership is built on clarity and trust, especially when it comes to ownership of creative work and sensitive materials. This Intellectual Property (IP) clause is designed to protect both of us, ensuring there is no ambiguity about who owns what. My fundamental principle is simple: your assets are yours, my assets are mine, and we both grant the rights necessary for a productive collaboration.

1. My Intellectual Property (The "Tools of My Trade")

I have developed proprietary systems, processes, and materials that allow me to deliver high-quality service efficiently. These assets remain my exclusive property.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Pre-existing Templates & Frameworks: Email templates, workflow checklists, standard operating procedures (SOPs), project management boards, and onboarding systems I created before our engagement.
  • Methodologies & Processes: The unique ways I organize information, manage projects, and optimize workflows.
  • Website & Marketing Content: All original text, copy, articles, graphics, and design elements on this website and associated marketing materials.

Why this matters: This protects the investment I have made in developing my business tools. You benefit from these refined systems, but the underlying IP remains with me to use in serving other clients.

2. Your Intellectual Property (Your "Crown Jewels")

Your business assets are sacred. Any materials, data, or confidential information you share with me remain 100% your property.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Brand Assets: Your logos, trademarks, brand names, color schemes, and brand guidelines.
  • Business Data: Your customer lists, prospect information, financial data, and internal documents.
  • Creative Content: Your website content, marketing copy, course materials, product designs, and any other content you created.
  • Confidential Information: Any trade secrets, business strategies, or proprietary information you disclose.

Why this matters: This guarantees that you retain complete ownership and control over everything that makes your business unique. I act as a steward of your IP, using it solely for the purpose of fulfilling our agreement.

3. Deliverables & Work Product (The "Finished Work")

This section defines the ownership of the specific outputs I create for you during our engagement.

  • Final Paid Work Product: Any specific, customized deliverables that I create for you and that you pay for (e.g., a designed report, a researched document, a curated list, a managed social media campaign) become your property upon full payment. You are free to use, modify, and distribute these deliverables as you see fit for your business.
  • My Underlying Systems & Frameworks: While the final deliverable is yours, the underlying, reusable systems, templates, or processes I may use or create to produce that deliverable remain my IP. However, I grant you a perpetual, royalty-free license to use those specific systems solely in conjunction with the deliverables you paid for.

Example for Clarity:

If I create a custom client onboarding process for you, the specific document with your company name and details is yours. The master template I developed and adapted for you remains my IP, but you have full lifetime rights to use your customized version.

4. Confidentiality & Mutual Respect

This IP framework is underpinned by a mutual commitment to confidentiality, as outlined in our broader agreement. I will:

  • Treat all of your intellectual property as strictly confidential.
  • Only use your materials for the explicit purpose of providing services to you.
  • Never replicate, repurpose, or resell your unique deliverables for another client.

This ensures clarity, protects both our interests, and fosters a relationship based on professional respect and trust. By defining these boundaries from the start, we can collaborate with confidence and focus entirely on achieving your business goals.
